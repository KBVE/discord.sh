## About the Game

BugWars throws you into a world where nature's smallest creatures become your biggest threat. Armed with determination and quick thinking, you must survive wave after wave of bug attacks. The question isn't if you'll fall - it's how long you can last.

Will you rise to the challenge, or will you become another victim of the Bug Wars?
